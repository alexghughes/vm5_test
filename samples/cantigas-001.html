Encoding Principles

Main text from M2 with variants from other editions as listed above. Line division as per lines or hemistichs of this edition; departures of structure in other editions noted in variants (V presents hemistichs as separate lines, so its division only remarked on when in conflict with M2). Any emendations of structure given by M2 noted in variants and indicated in main text by [[supplied text]]. C and A are musical editions, dependent for their text on M2 and V respectively; significant departures from respective source texts indicated, otherwise can be read as containing all variants listed for source text.

As editions vary in treatment of archaic forms, linguistic focus etc. departures from M2 text only recorded where considered significant. Use of i/j/y, u/v not recorded; however use of m/n, differences in word division and capitalization all included. Syllabification in musical editions is indicated by "_". Variation in refrain repeats: all departures from pattern of M2 are indicated; fluctuating forms in any of the sources are indicated, marked by "~", under the first refrain.

All variants given within the text.
